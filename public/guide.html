Creator Guide

Everything you need to know about working with BIG Records promos.

01
Log in to the portal
Go to app.bigrecords.com and enter the email we invited you with. You'll receive a 6-digit code — enter it to log in. No password needed.
Enter your email → receive a code → you're in
02
View your campaigns
The Promos tab shows all campaigns assigned to you. Each card shows the campaign name, payment amount, and deadline. Tap a card to see full details.
Your campaigns — active and completed
03
Accept or decline
New invites show Accept and Decline buttons. Accepting means you commit to posting a video using the campaign sound before the deadline.
⚠️ Only accept if you can deliver before the deadline. Campaigns that pass the deadline without a submission are automatically declined.
04
Post & submit your video
Create your TikTok video using the sound link from the campaign. Post it on TikTok, then open the campaign in the portal and paste your video URL.
Paste your TikTok video link after posting
💡 Make sure the sound in your video matches the campaign sound before submitting.
05
Campaign statuses
Each campaign goes through these stages:
Invited
You've been invited — accept or decline
Accepted
You accepted — post your video & submit the link
Posted
Video submitted — we're reviewing
Completed
Approved — payment added to your wallet
Declined
You declined or the deadline passed
06
Wallet & payments
The Wallet tab shows your balance and history. Once a campaign is completed, the payment is added to your balance. Tap Withdraw to request a PayPal payout.
Track your earnings and request withdrawals
07
Account settings
In the Account tab, save your PayPal email — this is where payments are sent. You can also access this guide and FAQ from there.
💡 Keep your PayPal email up to date. We can only send payments to the email saved in your account.
FAQ
Frequently asked questions
Can I change my mind after accepting?
You can't undo an acceptance through the portal. If you don't submit before the deadline, the campaign will auto-decline. Need to cancel early? Contact us.
What if I post the wrong sound?
We'll flag it during review. You may need to re-upload with the correct sound before the deadline.
How long until I get paid?
After your campaign is Completed, request a withdrawal in the Wallet tab. Payments are processed within 1–3 business days via PayPal.
Can I delete my TikTok video after getting paid?
No — videos must stay up permanently. Deleting a promo video may result in payment being revoked and removal from future campaigns.
I didn't receive the login code
Check your spam/junk folder. The email comes from [email protected]. Still nothing? DM us on Instagram.
How do I update my PayPal?
Go to the Account tab in the portal and save your new PayPal email anytime.
